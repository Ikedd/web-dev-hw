How to do Wood Burning.

This is the basics on how to start woodburning on certain types of wood. It is
a relatively cheap hobby to start and you can create some beautiful things.

All the supplies that you need which is only a few can be found at Lowe's or at Home Depot.
a woodburning kit and supplies will be fourd here.

Materials Needed:

  1. Wood-be creative, make a small jewelry box or tool box and wood burn something on that.
  2. Magnifying glass-any size really, but the bigger, the better(and faster)
  3. Stencil of what your going to woodburn-Print it out
  4. Pencil-To trace your stencil to the actual wood
  5. Dark Shades-Protect your eyes
  6. Wood burning tool kit
  7. Flat, Smooth piece of wood.
Step Summary of Parts
Step 1 Draw pattern on stencil Sheet.
Step 2 Put carbon paper under stencel on wood.
Step 3 Tape and Trace your pattern and start burning.
